# E-commerce-Sales-Performance-Market-Insights-Analysis-eBay-Perfume-Sector

Tools :SQL/Python/Power BI

Description:Problem & Objective: Addressed a noticeable decline in perfume sales for an eBay retailer by analyzing transactional data to identify underlying market drivers, evaluate product performance, and uncover growth opportunities.  

Data Preparation & Engineering: Cleaned, structured, and normalized multi-gender e-commerce datasets using SQL and Python (Pandas), streamlining columns and standardizing messy text fields (e.g., product categories and brands) for precise analytical modeling.
  
Hypothesis Testing & Insights: * Validated that premium/classic brands maintain an average sales volume 15x higher per brand compared to niche alternatives.  Used SQL window functions to segment items by median price , proving that high-end perfumes yield significantly superior profit margins per unit despite lower transactional frequency.  Debunked the assumption that Eau de Parfum outperforms Eau de Toilette, discovering instead that Eau de Toilette drove substantially larger total sales volumes.  

Visualization & Reporting: Built dynamic dashboards in Power BI to map the price-demand elasticity curve, clearly visualizing how sales peak at accessible price points. 
 
Business Value: Delivered data-driven recommendations on price optimization, stock distribution (prioritizing high-volume Eau de Toilette options), and distinct marketing strategies tailored for both premium and budget-friendly product segments.
